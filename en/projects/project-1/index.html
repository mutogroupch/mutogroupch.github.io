Hirschwiese Settlement Renewal

Zurich 2025 2nd phase, open competition

Architecture: Mohn Studio, Project Lead Bartlomiej Cybula

The residential ensemble is structured by a sequence of public open spaces formed by a play street and a central urban square. Together, they create the social core of the development and support shared use and interaction.

Three interconnected typologies – garden, urban square, and courtyard – offer diverse spatial qualities within a coherent design language. A planting strategy based on native species reinforces the identity of the place.
